Request Data Deletion

Justblr Matrix - Task Management App

What data we collect:

  • Reminders and tasks you create
  • Contact names associated with reminders
  • Device identifier for syncing

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete within the App

You can delete individual reminders and tasks directly in the Justblr Matrix app by swiping left on any item.

Option 2: Request Complete Data Deletion

To request deletion of all your data from our servers, please email us:

Email: support@justblrmatrix.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Include: Your device ID (found in app settings) or phone number associated with your reminders

Data Retention

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:

  • Delete all your reminders and tasks
  • Delete all contact information
  • Remove your device identifier
  • Complete deletion within 30 days

For any questions, contact us at support@justblrmatrix.com
