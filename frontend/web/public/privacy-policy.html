Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Justblr Matrix ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Contact Information: With your permission, we access your device contacts to help you create reminders for specific people. This data is stored locally and synced to our secure servers for cross-device functionality.

Reminder Data: We store the reminders, tasks, and notes you create within the app.

Device Information: We collect a unique device identifier to sync your data across devices.

Voice Data: When you use voice commands, audio is processed to convert speech to text. We do not store voice recordings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To send scheduled notifications and reminders
  • To improve our app and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your information.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services (WhatsApp, SMS, Phone) to execute actions. When you initiate these actions, the respective app's privacy policy applies.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
